The Best Movies According to Astrid

My top 3 movies of all time

Romancing the Stone

This is a classic adventure movie that has all the elements that I love. There's exotic locations, secrets in the jungle, the hunt for a precious stone, ancient profecies, and - of course - there is a love story that has a good ending. On top of that it has a good dose of humor and great actors.

The Abyss

This is a sci-fi movie that takes place in the deepest oceans, a place that has not been explored by humans. I love movies that are about ocean discoveries, adventure, and a great alien discovery. Good actors, strong story, and edge of your seat excitement.

Shawshank Redemption

Amazing actors, fantastic story line, and simply a classic that has to be in everyone's top 3.
